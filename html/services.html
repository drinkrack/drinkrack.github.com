We're ready to Serve you with best

DrinkRack provides a hassle-free beverage solution tailored to your office needs. We offer 20+ types of beverages, regularly updating our selection to keep things fresh and exciting.

Our beverage racks come in different sizes to suit offices of differnet scales. Whether you have a small team of 5 or a large workplace with 30+ employees, we provide the perfect setup. With adjustable capacities ranging from 2 to 6 crate slots, our racks ensure flexibility as your team grows. Designed with a modern aesthetic, they are built with sturdy wood and durable stainless steel for long-lasting stability and reliability.

We handle everything—delivery, setup, and regular restocking—so your team in Berlin, Germany always has access to their favorite drinks without any hassle.
