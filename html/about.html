About Us

The Ultimate Refreshment Station for Your Office!

The Ultimate Refreshment Station for Your Office!
At DrinkRack, we make office refreshments effortless with a hassle-free beverage solution. Founded in 2025 and operating in Berlin, our mission is to provide workplaces with an easy, cost-free way to keep employees refreshed and energized throughout the day.

There are no subscriptions or commitments—start anytime and stop anytime with complete flexibility. Your company incurs no costs, as employees only pay for what they consume. Payments are simple and trust-based—just scan the QR code and pay instantly.

We take care of everything, from setting up the drink rack to keeping it stocked, so there's no extra effort required on your end. With a wide selection of beverages at supermarket prices, our compact and flexible racks fit seamlessly into any office space. We monitor stock levels and refill regularly, ensuring your team always has access to their favorite drinks. If the rack runs low unexpectedly, just let us know, and we'll restock it quickly.

Our Story

DrinkRack was founded by two computer science students from TU and HU Berlin, Mohammad Orabe and Sedra Abou Ghaloun. We realized how inconvenient it was for office workers to find affordable drinks without stepping out of the workplace. So, we came up with a simple, trust-based beverage solution that fits any office, with no subscriptions or commitments. Today, DrinkRack helps businesses across Berlin keep their teams refreshed effortlessly.

In the future, we want to take it a step further by integrating IoT technology into our racks. This will allow us to track stock levels in real time, automate restocking, and make the whole experience even more seamless for offices.
